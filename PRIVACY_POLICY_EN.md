# Privacy Policy

**NERVE** (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your personal information and is committed to complying with all applicable laws and regulations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## 1. Personal Information Collected
The App collects the minimum amount of personal information necessary to provide seamless services:

- **Login and Profile Information**: Nickname, User Identification Value (UUID)
- **Service Usage Records**: Login timestamps, savings records (category, amount, notes), wishlist information (title, price, creation date, achievement status, etc.)
- **Device Information**: Device identifier (Device ID), OS version, push tokens for notification services
- **Other**: Neural Efficiency & Reward Verification Engine data (success/failure records, penalty amounts, etc.)

## 2. Purpose of Collection and Use
The collected information is used for the following purposes:

- **Service Functionality**: Providing personalized savings and wishlist management features
- **Service Improvement and Optimization**: Improving the application's efficiency (Neural Efficiency) through user pattern analysis
- **User Management**: Prevention of unauthorized use and identity verification
- **Notification Features**: Sending push notifications related to savings goals and achievement status

## 3. Third-Party Disclosure and Entrustment
To provide stable services, the App utilizes external services and entrusts the processing of personal information as follows:

| Provider | Purpose | Information Provided |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Supabase** | Data storage and user authentication | User ID, Nickname, Service usage data |
| **Firebase (Google)** | Push notifications and app analytics | Push tokens, Device information, Usage records |
| **Google Gemini AI** | Data analysis and personalization | Wishlist and savings-related text data |

## 4. Retention and Usage Period
- Your personal information is retained until **account deletion** or **service termination**, after which it is destroyed without delay.
- However, if retention is required by applicable laws, the information will be stored for the period specified by such laws.

## 5. User Rights and How to Exercise Them
Users can check or modify their personal information at any time through the App settings and can withdraw consent to the use of personal information by deleting the App.

## 6. Efforts to Protect Personal Information
The App makes every effort to protect user data by using data encryption and secure communication (HTTPS).

## 7. Revisions and Notification Obligations
This policy may change according to service policy or legal requirements. Significant changes will be notified through the App.
